About Me

A Registered Nurse!

Nurses play a crucial role in healthcare by providing compassionate and comprehensive care to patients. Their responsibilities include acting as caregivers, advocates, educators, communicators, leaders, and researchers, ensuring patients receive proper treatment and support. Their functions involve assessing and diagnosing health conditions, planning and implementing care strategies, and evaluating patient progress. Nursing care encompasses holistic, compassionate, preventive, rehabilitative, and end-of-life support, addressing both physical and emotional needs. By maintaining high ethical and professional standards, nurses contribute significantly to patient safety, recovery, and overall well-being, making them essential to the healthcare system..
